Automated Skin Disease Detection Using Deep Learning

About

To develop an AI-based system that can detect common skin diseases from images using deep learning.

Skin disease classification is the process of identifying different types of skin conditions. It can be done using deep learning models, such as convolutional neural networks

Selecting Skin Diseases

We will focus on detecting 7 common skin conditions:

Acne and Rosacea Eczema Psoriasis Melanoma BCC - Basal Cell Carcinoma Ringworm
Pimples, blackheads, and clogged pores and – Facial redness with visible blood vessels. Dry, red, itchy skin. Scaly patches due to immune system issues. A serious form of skin cancer. A slow-growing type of skin cancer. A fungal infection causing a circular rash.
